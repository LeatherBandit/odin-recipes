All American Roast Beef

Description

Roast beef is a classic main dish for holidays, family get-togethers, and elegant dinners.

This incredibly easy-to-follow, 4-ingredient recipe will help you create a flavorful, moist, and tender roast like no other

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef eye of round roast, at room temperature
  • ½ teaspoon kosher sale, or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ℉ (190 ℃)

  2. Make sure roast is at room temperature. Tie roast at 3-inch intervals with cotton twine; this helps it from drying out and allows it to cook more evenly. Place roast on a rack in baking pan.

  3. Mix together salt, garlic powder, and pepper in a small bowl. Rub seasoning mixture into meat.

  4. Place in preheated oven and roast for 60 minutes, or 20 minutes per pound if your roast is larger or smaller. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 120 to 125 degrees ℉ (49 to 52 ℃)

  5. Remove from the oven and transfer to a cutting board. Cover loosely with foil, and let rest so juices can redistribute, 15 to 20 minutes.

  6. Slice and serve.
